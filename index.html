I am a Data Scientist and a graduate student in Physics, aspiring to transition my career into Astrophysics and Astrophotography. Currently, I am pursuing a second Master's degree at the University of Lethbridge, having previously earned an M.S. in Computer Science from the University of Texas at Dallas and a B.Tech. in Information Technology from West Bengal University of Technology. With several years of experience as a Data Scientist and Software Engineer, I specialize in developing advanced astronomical models that leverage machine learning techniques, particularly in anomaly detection. My recent research has led me to explore the intricacies of Transition-Edge-Sensor (TES) Detector Systems and Double Fourier Interferometry, which are the integral components of my Master's thesis at U Lethbridge.

Check out my website for more details!
